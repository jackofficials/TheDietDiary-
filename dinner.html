Delicious Lemon Herb Baked Chicken with Roasted Vegetables Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, carrots)
  • 1 teaspoon fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions:
  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken breasts, coating them well. Let them marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare the Vegetables: Chop the mixed vegetables and toss them with a little olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Arrange in Baking Dish: Place the marinated chicken in a baking dish and surround it with the prepared vegetables.
  5. Bake: Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm. Enjoy!
Serving Suggestion:

This lemon herb chicken can be paired with a side of quinoa or a fresh garden salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Delicious Vegetable and Chickpea Curry Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Heat the Oil: In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add Garlic and Ginger: Stir in the minced garlic and ginger, cooking for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Vegetables: Add the bell pepper, carrots, and zucchini. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables start to soften.
  4. Add Chickpeas and Spices: Stir in the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, coconut milk, curry powder, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Simmer: Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired. Serve warm with rice or naan.
Serving Suggestion:

This vegetable and chickpea curry pairs wonderfully with steamed rice or warm naan bread for a complete and hearty meal.

This flavorful Vegetable and Chickpea Curry is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it a perfect dinner option!

Delicious Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 8 oz spaghetti or your choice of pasta
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
Instructions:
  1. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the shrimp, seasoning with salt and pepper, and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, add the remaining butter and minced garlic. Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant, then add red pepper flakes if using.
  4. Combine Pasta and Shrimp: Add the cooked pasta and shrimp back into the skillet, tossing to combine. If the mixture seems dry, add reserved pasta water a little at a time until you reach your desired consistency.
  5. Add Lemon and Parsley: Stir in lemon zest, lemon juice, and chopped parsley, mixing well to coat everything evenly.
  6. Serve: Divide the pasta among plates, garnishing with extra parsley and lemon wedges if desired.
Serving Suggestion:

This Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta pairs beautifully with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete meal.

This flavorful Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta is quick to prepare and makes for a perfect dinner, combining delicious flavors with a nutritious profile!

Delicious Stuffed Bell Peppers with Ground Turkey and Quinoa Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (mozzarella or cheddar, optional)
  • Olive oil for sautéing
Instructions:
  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Bell Peppers: Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds. Drizzle with a little olive oil and place them in a baking dish.
  3. Sauté the Filling: In a skillet, heat a little olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion and garlic, sautéing until soft. Add ground turkey, cooking until browned. Stir in cooked quinoa, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stuff the Peppers: Fill each bell pepper with the turkey and quinoa mixture, pressing down gently to pack it in. If using, top with shredded cheese.
  5. Bake: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted.
Serving Suggestion:

Serve warm, garnished with fresh herbs or a dollop of sour cream for added flavor.

This Stuffed Bell Peppers with Ground Turkey and Quinoa recipe is not only delicious but also packed with nutrition, making it a perfect dinner choice for any occasion!
